All is well, it happens to the best of us.

While you're here, enjoy a nice minute-long somatic practice to help you find a calm space within you.

  • Sit comfortably, or lie down. Feel the connection of your body to the surface beneath you. Can you feel supported by the ground?
  • Relax your eyes, as you become aware of the connection of the eyes to the neck, to the spine and the whole nervous system.
  • Bring awareness to the center of your chest, your heart, and imagine the apex of the heart on the right, and the bottom of the heart on the left, softening.
  • Notice the sensation around the abdomen. What is present in the belly? Allow information to flow from the abdomen to the brain and back.
  • Take 3 deeper breaths - in through the nose, and out through the mouth. Let all the air out, you have time.
  • Keep scanning your body for a few more moments, with your eyes closed.
