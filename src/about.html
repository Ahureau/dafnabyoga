About me

Yoga and the start of my journey

The human body communicates in its own unique way, and my greatest passion is learning to understand it. My journey with body-mind work began with movement. I started dancing at a very young age and have always cherished the magic of expressing life through the body.

At 20 years old, I discovered yoga and have never stopped practicing since. Yoga and meditation have been guiding my personal and professional paths, and they have been a true gift for me. With over 900 hours of yoga training, I have had the fortune of studying with experienced and devoted yoga teachers from all over the world. I taught for five years in Tel Aviv, where I held beginner and advanced workshops, assisted in teacher trainings, and mentored new teachers.

Reflexology and a new path

I studied reflexology for two years and, according to the Israeli standard, I am a senior reflexologist. As a teenager, reflexology miraculously balanced my hormonal system with just a few short treatments. I have always greatly appreciated the method, and it has proven powerful in my bodywork sessions.

Following an inner call for change, I moved to Berlin in 2020 and stayed there for four years. I continued teaching yoga in person and online, and added the Body Mind Centering¬Æ method to my toolbox. Gradually, my vinyasa-yoga classes became more focused on somatic work and deep, calm body awareness.
