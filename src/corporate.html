Description

Yoga at work is a wonderful opportunity for people to connect, move and relax. I noticed the positive influence of these classes when I was teaching in various companies, such as Google, PayPal, Melio, Dell and more.

In our fast-paced yet sedentary life, it is essential to keep the body mobile and the nervous system regulated.

I would love to share all the gifts of yoga in your work place, too.
