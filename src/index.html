About

Highlights from a lifetime journey in movement and body therapy

Hi, I'm Dafna

My passion is helping you connect with your body’s unique language.

From a young age, dance, yoga, and meditation have shaped my life, guiding me on a path of deep body-mind awareness. With over 900 hours of yoga training and years of experience, I offer a variety of approaches in my yoga classes.

“The body has the ability to heal itself” is one of the main mottos of natural medicine, which I try to follow in my practice and life. Based on this ideal, I offer holistic bodywork sessions that combine my knowledge in reflexology and osteopathy. By helping bring the physiology of your body back in balance, we will bring ease and peace to your life.

After enriching my practice in Tel Aviv and Berlin, I now bring my holistic approach to Groningen. Let’s explore together how touch, movement, body awareness, and breath can enhance your connection to yourself. My goal is to support and empower you, making your journey softer, healthier, and happier.

Looking forward to meeting you and sharing this path to wellbeing.

Namaste,

Dafna
