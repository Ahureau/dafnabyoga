Hi, I'm Dafna

I’ve been exploring the world of movement for over twenty years, and have been dedicating myself to yoga, meditation, the wisdom of the human body and holistic medicine for over a decade.

Yoga is a path that allows us to feel whole and close to our deepest truth.

“The body has the ability to heal itself” is the main motto in reflexology, which I try to follow in my practice and life.

Using touch, movement, meditation and breath techniques holistic bodywork can address pain and diseases in the different systems of the body - external & internal, physical & mental.

I wish to live life fully and am here to share this joy with others. We are entitled to experience true acceptance - of ourselves, of everyone around us, of the present moment.

Let me help in supporting, developing, and empowering you, to make your journey softer, healthier, happier.
