While you're here, enjoy my favorite pranayama practice to strengthen the lungs: Viloma pranayama 1.

  • Find a comfortable seat, and root down with the pelvis
  • Once the body is comfortable and at ease, bring awareness to your breath
  • In this technique we divide our inhalations to 3 parts, from bottom to top
  • At the end of the inhalations hold the breath while gently lifting the pelvic floor
  • Exhale fully
  • Take one recovery breath - normal breath in and out
  • Start again with 3 part inhalation
  • Keep a stable rhythm and follow this pattern for 5 minutes
  • Add 5 minutes of silent observation
